It takes a lot of effort to earn money. The only way to safeguard it is to learn how.





Have you ever heard of terms like ‘he lost his money in the stock market? Mutual funds are subject to market risks?


There is a famous chinese saying ‘give a man a fish and you feed him for a day, teach a man to fish and you feed him for a lifetime’. Since the financial industry began, people have been victims of fraudulent and unethical practices in the market.

After taking this course of one month a learner will be able to become self-sufficient in matters of personal finance handling. The course covers everything from risk assessment to management of personal finance.

Outline of the course:

  • HOUR 1: Overview(personal finance)
  • HOUR 2: Banking, accounts, Loans and Credit Cards
  • HOUR 3: Overview on smart Investment and Financial Planning
  • HOUR 4: Debt Investment - Non risky
  • HOUR 5: Stock Market I (Overview : How to invest in stock market)
  • HOUR 6: Stock Market II (How to safeguard yourself in stock market)
  • HOUR 7: Mutual fund I (Overview on mutual fund investing)
  • HOUR 8: Mutual Fund II (Pitfalls of mutual fund investing)
  • HOUR 9: Real estate
  • HOUR 10: Gold
  • HOUR 11: Insurance
  • HOUR 12: Income Tax
  • HOUR 13: Standard Investment Practices
  • HOUR 14: Financial Frauds and Risks
  • HOUR 15: Will and inheritance
  • HOUR 16: Recap + where is the future?

It takes a lot of effort to earn money. Safeguard yourself from market frauds and learn basics of investment, banking, insurances and pitfalls of different financial products. Invest 16 Hours to become self-sufficient in making wise decisions for your personal finance and investments.

Have you ever heard things like ‘he lost his money in the stock market, mutual funds are subject to market risks, tax liability incurred on investments‘?


Have you ever wondered what this all means?

There is a famous Chinese saying:

Give a man a fish and you feed him for a day, teach a man to fish and you feed him for a lifetime.

Since the financial industry began, people have been victims of fraudulent and unethical practices in the market. Investkeen takes pride in teaching people how to ‘fish’.

After taking this course of one month a learner will be able to become self-sufficient in matters of personal finance handling.

The course syllabus covers everything from risk assessment to management of personal finance. No more dependence on second-hand information from anybody.

Outline of the course:

  • HOUR 1: Overview(personal finance)
  • HOUR 2: Banking, accounts, Loans and Credit Cards
  • HOUR 3: Overview on smart Investment and Financial Planning
  • HOUR 4: Debt Investment - Non risky
  • HOUR 5: Stock Market I (Overview : How to invest in stock market)
  • HOUR 6: Stock Market II (How to safeguard yourself in stock market)
  • HOUR 7: Mutual fund I (Overview on mutual fund investing)
  • HOUR 8: Mutual Fund II (Pitfalls of mutual fund investing)
  • HOUR 9: Real estate
  • HOUR 10: Gold
  • HOUR 11: Insurance
  • HOUR 12: Income Tax
  • HOUR 13: Standard Investment Practices
  • HOUR 14: Financial Frauds and Risks
  • HOUR 15: Will and inheritance
  • HOUR 16: Recap + where is the future?

Duration : One month
Classes on: Saturdays and Sundays (2 hours each day)
Classes commence : 1 OCT 2016
Total number of hours : 16
Batch size : 12 people.
Mode of Delivery of course : Classroom lectures and visualizations along with Q&A sessions.

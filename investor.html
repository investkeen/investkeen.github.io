Investkeen understands that every investor is different with their own investment style.



We understand that real estate, gold, ETFs, mutual funds, stocks and fixed deposits are preferred investment instruments in India. We also understand that investment in real estate is traditionally for personal living arrangements, investment in gold goes as a cultural compulsion, and so on for every asset class but when it comes to investment in stocks we look at it as a loss making mechanism. We consider them an asset class largely being manipulated, an asset class which is the favourite asset class of gamblers. However, over the the last century, it is the asset class which has given maximum returns year by year. The fact that many scams, unethical practices and nuisances are associated with it is also true. So, we need to be very careful in handling our investments in stocks.

We, at Investkeen, help you identify stocks which are creating value in the present so we can predict the future intelligently. We also identify risks associated with them and suggest you how to mitigate those risks such that they give you maximum returns within your comfortable time frame.

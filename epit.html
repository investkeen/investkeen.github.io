Executive Program in Investment and Trading
(EPIT)


Are you looking to build a career in the stock market? If yes, then this course will give you a platform. EPIT is designed to expose students to different areas of stock market trading. We cover topics ranging from fundamentals of financial markets upto becoming a good advisor and trader in the investment banking domain. Classes are both theoretical and practical.

The course syllabus is inclusive of Capital Market (Dealers) Module (CMDM) and Derivative Market (Dealers) Module (DMDM) required by NSE to become a trader.



What are the prerequisites for this course?

Willingness to learn with an open mind. You should be good with numbers. No prior knowledge of financial management / commerce / higher mathematics is required. .

Career prospects after course:

We will conduct a final test. 100% money back guarantee if you score more than 75% on the test. Also, you will get a chance to have an interview with us (Investkeen Capital) for a job of 4-8 lpa depending on your performance. We will make you capable to clear both NCFM modules certified by NSE (National Stock Exchange of India) and can easily apply for a trader role in any stock broking house.

Course Structure


Part 1 | Functioning of Indian market

  1. Stock market & Trading overview.
  2. Five Stock market participants overview - Investors, Stock exchange, Stock brokers, SEBI, Depository.
  3. Four types of assets and their trading overview.
    • Stock Trading:- Intraday Trading, Delivery based trading
    • Derivative market:- Futures and Option
    • Currency trading:- Indian currency market
    • Commodities trading:- Gold, Silver, Crude oil, Agri commodities.
  4. Elementary mathematics for Stock market.
  5. Live trading sessions training in all four markets. (Stock, Derivative, Currency, Commodity)

In this part of the training you will get inside-out exposure of Indian stock market, its functioning, participants, different products and trading platforms. You will get to know why the stock market exists and what are the different types of work carried out in the industry.

Part 2 | Becoming a good advisor and trader

  1. How to Identify good shares?
  2. How to make money trading options?
  3. How to make a good Investment Portfolio?
  4. How to manage the risk of Share market?
  5. How to manage currency risk for importers and exporters and start a business in Currency Hedging?

In this part of the training we will teach you how to be a good participant in this industry as an advisor, trader or entrepreneur.

Part 3 | Capital Market (Dealers) Module

  1. An overview of the Indian Securities Market
  2. Trading Membership
  3. Trading
  4. Clearing and Settlement
  5. Legal Framework
  6. Fundamental valuation concept

    7. In this part of the training each and every concept of CMDM module will be covered.

Part 4 | Derivative Market (Dealers) Module

  1. Introduction to Derivatives
  2. Understanding Interest Rates and Stock Indices
  3. Future Contracts, Mechanism and Pricing Forward Contracts
  4. Application of Futures Contracts
  5. Options Contracts, Mechanism and Applications
  6. Pricing of Options Contracts and Greek Letters
  7. Trading of Derivative Contracts
  8. Clearing and Settlement
  9. Regulatory Framework
  10. Accounting for Derivatives

In this part of the training each and every concept of DMDM module will be covered.
